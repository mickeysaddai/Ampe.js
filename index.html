Instructions

Inspired by the real game Ampe, Ampe.js is designed to be played against computer.

The goal of the game is to eliminate all of the opponen't players. To start the game off, the starting players on both teams will each choose a color. Team Match(the human player) will manually pick a color between the two colors in the game and Team Unmatch(the computer player) will randomly generate a color between the two colors as well.


If the colors match, the current Winner of Team Match Team will play against the next opponent of Team Unmactch. This will be denoted by a point on the scoreboard for Team Match. Likewise, if the colors do not match, the current Winner of Team Unmatch will then play againt the next opponent of Team Match. Whoever gets to the last player and wins against that oppononet eliminates that opponent from the game, thereby reducing the number of opponents that will be playing in the next round. Once the last player is eliminated(denoted by black), a new round begins.

The round starts again and continues until all the players of an opponent's team are killed. The remaining team standing becomes the WINNER.
