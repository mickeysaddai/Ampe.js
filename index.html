Welcome To Ampe.JS

Instructions

Ampe.js is designed to be played against computer.

The player wins points for when their chosen color matches the computer's randomly generated color. For every point won, the winning player proceeds to play with their next opponent. The last player of the opponent's team gets killed when they lose their turn(indicated by black).

The round starts again and continues until all the players of an opponent's team are killed. The remaining team standing becomes the WINNER.
